Add two improvements to the Worker Place Bid page.

Context:
The worker New Jobs flow is now using /workers/jobs/new. Each job has latitude/longitude/address/status fields. On the Place Bid page, I want the worker to see a small map preview of the job location near the top.

- Show a marker at the job location.
- Show the address text below or above the map.
- Keep the map height around 180–220px.

4. Keep changes minimal
